## Overview

SoundMixer is a simple ambient sound mixer app. We respect your privacy completely.

## Data Collection

**We collect no data. Zero. None.**

- No analytics
- No tracking
- No crash reporting
- No personal information
- No account creation
- No server communication

## Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (selected sounds, volume levels, timer presets) locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Can be cleared at any time through your device settings

## Third-Party Services

SoundMixer does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Network Access

SoundMixer does not require or use network access. The app functions entirely offline.

## Children's Privacy

SoundMixer does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this document. But honestly, our policy is simple: we don't collect your data, and we never will.
